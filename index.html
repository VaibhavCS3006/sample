Guaranteed Rent

Unique Guaranteed Rent scheme is designed to represent the ultimate Property Management service, making the entire process of securing a tenancy and collecting rent as simple and secure as possible. This is our way of providing peace of mind for landlords.

With Guaranteed Rent, the landlord receives the agreed rental income every month on an established date. No late payments and no missed payments, regardless of whether or not the tenant has paid. We eliminate the risk of a non-payment by guaranteeing that the rental income will be there, no matter what.
